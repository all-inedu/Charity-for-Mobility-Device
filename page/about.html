ABOUT US

The Physically Handicapped People's Charity, or PHPC, is an organisation that collects donations in the form of mobile devices, such as crutches and wheelchairs, from the general public and donateseach device to people in need who apply and order a device from our website.



WHAT WE DO

The PHPC aims to provide mobility devices such as wheelchairs, crutches, and the like by raising funds and purchasing working used or new devices such as functioning but unused crutches. These devices can be donated to our organsation through a website, and the PHPC can distribute these to people who are in need of these devices but are unable to obtain them.
